关于CNIOT和IIOT (About CNIOT and IIOT)

The IIOT is a young project which aims to be just like the International Olympiad in Informatics, but the problems are meant to be solved in a team. IIOT 是一个新兴且很有意思的项目，其目标是与国际信息学奥林匹克竞赛 (IOI) 类似，但其题目需要团队来解决。
The participants are teams of 4 high school students who represent a school (like in ICPC). 参赛者是每组 4 名高中学生组成的团队，他们会代表一所学校（类似于ICPC和CCPC）。
(According to the IIOT official website: https://iio.team/)
（来自 IIOT 官方网站: https://iio.team/）
CNIOT is a route for Chinese students to enter the IIOT official contest. CNIOT 是中国学生参加 IIOT 正式比赛的通道。

为啥要团队 (Why in teams?)

  • Teamwork is a requirement in the labor market. 团队合作在职业中很重要
  • Encourages mutual support and learning. 互相支持和学习（还有可能有大佬帮助）
  • Promotes creativity which comes from the comparison of different ideas. 各种各样的想法促进创造力和脑洞
  • Students will be more willing to participate in similar competitions (NOIP-NOI-IOI, ICPC). 更愿意参加其他的信息学比赛，包括高中与大学阶段。
From the IIOT official website: https://iio.team/
来自IIOT官方网站，还请多多支持: https://iio.team/

参与资格 (Eligibility)

  • An official participant should be a middle/high school student (grade 6 - grade 12). 选手必须是初中或高中学生（等同6至12年级，各地区表述方式不同）
  • An official participant should be either one of the following: 选手必须满足如下条件：
    1) A permanent resident of Mainland China/Hong Kong/Macao/Taiwan.
    2) A citizen of Mainland China/Hong Kong/Macao/Taiwan.
    3) Studying in a middle/high school in Mainland China/Hong Kong/Macao/Taiwan.
    1) 中国大陆/香港/澳门/台湾地区的永久居民。
    2) 中国大陆/香港/澳门/台湾地区的公民。
    3) 在中国大陆/香港/澳门/台湾地区的一所中学/高中就读。
  • Participants can only join one team. 选手只能一次加入一支队伍（你总不能分身吧）
  • Teams that are composed of participants from at most 3 different schools/institution are allowed (the schools/institutions can be different in their geological position). 由来自最多 3 所不同学校/机构的参与者组成的团队是被允许参加的（这些学校/机构在地理位置上可以有所不同）。
  • A team should have at most 2 NOI Gold or IOI Gold/Silver medalist. 一个团队最多只能有两名获得过 NOI 金牌或 IOI 金牌/银牌的选手。
